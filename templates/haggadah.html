The Haggadah is the book that is read during the Passover seder. This book is really important and is used to pass the story of Passover down from one generation to another so that the story and its meaning stays alive. The book has stories along with prayers and pictures. During the Passover dinner everyone has their own Haggadah and is able to follow along.

Fun fact:The Haggadah was originally written in Hebrew and is now translated into many different languages. Hebrew is read from right to left, so you want to make sure you open the book in the opposite direction as most people are used to.
