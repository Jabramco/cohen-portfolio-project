Passover is a great celebration that happens every year. Familys around the world gather around to eat together and tell the story of the ancient Jews of Egypt who freed themselves from slavery.

The Plagues

The story goes that after the Pharaoh of Egypt refused to free the Jews from slavery, God inflicted 10 plagues on Egypt. The last plague was the worst. It was the death of all Egyptian first born kids.
